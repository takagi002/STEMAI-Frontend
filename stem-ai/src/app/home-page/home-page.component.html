What is STEM-AI?

STEM-AI is your new personal recommender. It sees what classes you are struggling in and recommends you tutoring while you can still improve your grade. Our algorithm takes data from past students in your class, sees how tutoring improved their grade, and recommends tutoring for you. We work closely with the STEM Center and your professors to provide you the help you need. Your Professors will also be able to identify when you are struggling in a class earlier in the course. When they see you struggling, they can send you an email recommending you get tutoring. With the help of STEM-AI, everyone can identify early on when to get help and from that we can connect you with tutors.
