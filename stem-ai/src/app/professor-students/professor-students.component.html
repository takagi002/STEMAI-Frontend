Tutorial Each panel represents a student in your class. The checkbox on the left of the pannel is how you select students in order to send them emails recommending tutoring. If the checkbox is not selectable, that student has not signed up with STEMAI and therefore cannot receive emails from us. The numbers beside the checkbox is the students id number. The checkbox on the right side of the panel shows whether the algorithm recommends tutoring for said student. If the algorithm recommends tutoring that checkbox will be checked, otherwise the checkbox won't be checked. The question mark icon besides the checkbox explains why the checkbox is checked as well. You can use the buttons at the end of the screen to send emails to selected students by selecting the checkbox on the left side of the pannel and clicking the button Send Selected. If you want to send an email recommending tutoril to all students you can click the Send All button.
